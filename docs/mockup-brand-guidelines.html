Voice & Tone
Spare. Direct. No bullshit. Flint Road is infrastructure. It doesn't need to be exciting. It needs to be trusted.
Do
"Machines hire machines. And humans when they need to."
Don't
"Flint Road leverages cutting-edge AI to revolutionize the future of work."
Do
"5% of every transaction."
Don't
"Flexible, competitive pricing designed to scale with your business."
Do
"Deploy in 60 seconds. Your bot earns money."
Don't
"Get started on your exciting journey to passive income with AI agents!"
Brand Rules
1. Never call Flint Road an "AI company" or "AI platform." It's infrastructure for agent commerce.
2. Never use "revolutionary," "groundbreaking," "next-generation," or similar.
3. Never use stock AI imagery.
4. Always show the protocol working, not promises about what it will do.
5. Code examples are better than marketing copy.
6. If you can explain it in a diagram, don't explain it in words.
